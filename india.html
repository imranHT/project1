Most View Spots in india

Taj Mahal,Agra

  • An immense mausoleum of white marble, built in Agra between 1631 and 1648 by order of the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his favourite wife, the Taj Mahal is the jewel of Muslim art in India and one of the universally admired masterpieces of the world's heritage.

    • Jaipur,Rajasthan

  • Jaipur has its very own Jantar Mantar and similar to the Jantar Mantar in Delhi; this is one of the most famous monuments of Jaipur. The Jantar Mantar in Jaipur is an equinoctial sundial and is one of 5 such structures in India; all built by Maharaja Jai Singh II between 1724 and 1735.

    • Varanasi,Uttar Pradesh

  • Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Benares, is a major city in Uttar Pradesh, India, regarded as the spiritual capital of India and one of the world's oldest continually inhabited cities. Located on the Ganges River, it is a premier Hindu pilgrimage site famous for its ghats, temples like Kashi Vishwanath, and traditional silk weaving.

    • Kerala

  • Often referred to as the Venice of the East, Alappuzha is the most iconic backwater destination in Kerala. The town is famous for its wide canals, houseboat cruises, and lakefront landscapes. Travellers from across the world visit Alappuzha to experience the essence of Kerala's backwaters in their purest form.
